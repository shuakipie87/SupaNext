# ChatGPT Prompts for Legal & Privacy

Use these prompts to generate your Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. Remember to review these with a legal professional.

## Prompt 1: Terms of Service
> "I am building a SaaS application called [APP_NAME] that provides [DESCRIPTION]. The pricing is subscription-based ([PRICING_DETAILS]). I need a professional Terms of Service agreement that covers user accounts, payments, cancellations, intellectual property, and limitation of liability. The company is based in [LOCATION]."

## Prompt 2: Privacy Policy
> "Generate a Privacy Policy for [APP_NAME]. We collect user emails, names, and payment information via Stripe. We use cookies for authentication and PostHog for analytics. We use Supabase for our database and Resend for emails. We do not sell user data. Ensure it is GDPR and CCPA compliant."

## Prompt 3: Cookie Policy
> "Write a short Cookie Policy for my website explaining that we only use essential cookies for authentication and session management, and one analytics cookie for PostHog."
